Contact & Feedback

The Pantheios homepage is http://www.pantheios.org/.

The Pantheios project is hosted on SourceForge. (http://www.pantheios.org/ resolves to http://pantheios.sourceforge.net/).

The following options are available for report bugs, requesting features, or contacting the team members for any other reasons. The options are listed in descending order of preference for the team.

  • For reporting bugs use:
    • the project Bug Tracker, or
    • the STLSoft newsgroup (prefixing your message with [Pantheios]).
  • For reporting feature requests use:
    • the Feature Request Tracker, or
    • the STLSoft newsgroup (prefixing your message with [Pantheios]).
  • For offering patches use:
    • the Patch Tracker, or
    • the STLSoft newsgroup (prefixing your message with [Pantheios]).
  • For general help use:
    • the Help Forum,
    • the pantheios-users Mailing List, or
    • the STLSoft newsgroup (prefixing your message with [Pantheios]).
  • For general discussion use:
    • the pantheios-users Mailing List, or
    • the Open Discussion Forum, or
    • the STLSoft newsgroup (prefixing your message with [Pantheios]).
  • For reporting problems with the STLSoft libraries use:
    • the STLSoft newsgroup (prefixing your message with [Pantheios]), or
    • email Matthew (see below).

If you need to email Matthew, you can do so via the following (obfuscated) address:

For mutual convenience, please prefix your email subject with [Pantheios].
